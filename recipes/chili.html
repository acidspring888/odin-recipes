Chili

Description

Chili has always been my favortie food, although it is not always good. I consider myself a chili connoisseur, if somewhere has chili, I have probably tried it. With that being said, really good chili is few and far between.

Since I am from the south, chili has always been around. Chili cook-offs, everyone has "World Famous Chili", and you haven't had real chili "'till you tried ma's". Simply following this recipe will not guarantee a great chili because it takes more than just reading and following directions. But with time and effort you can have your own "World Famous Chili"!

Ingredients

  • 2 lb Ground beef
  • 3 cups beef broth
  • 1 tomato
  • 1 onion
  • 1 jalepeno
  • 2 cans of kidney beans (light or dark)
  • 2 cans of tomato sauce
  • 1 tub of Bloomer's chili base
  • Preferred seasonings

Steps

  1. Cut tomato, onion, and jalepeno.
  2. Cook beef in pan and season how you like.
  3. In a large pot, add beef broth, tomato sauce, tomato, onion, jalepeno, kidney beans, chili base, and mix.
  4. Let this pot sit and add beef when it is cooked.
  5. Let simmer for two hours and enjoy.
