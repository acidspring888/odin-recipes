Chicken Wings

Description

Everybody loves chicken wings! They are quick and easy, and usually kids won't fuss much when given the option. At least mine doesn't. With some milk, egg, and flour, you can make your own chicken wings.

Ingredients

  • Boneless skinless chicken breasts
  • Flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 Eggs
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Onion powder
  • Garlic Powder
  • Any preferred seasonings really

Steps

  1. Cut breasts into cubes
  2. Crack the eggs and whisk together with milk
  3. Add some seasoning to the egg and milk mix
  4. Pour some flour in a gallon ziploc bag
  5. Add some seasoning with the flour
  6. Toss chicken in egg mix and then flour
  7. Your choice of frying method can vary, I prefer to air fry my chicken wings.
